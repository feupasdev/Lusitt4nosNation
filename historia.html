The Legacy

L4N V1

L4N was founded in 21/06/2025 by jota? and W1TAPZ (Now Currently feupas) soon they finded themselves at their peak, where with a complete 5 man roster, they started winning games, but soon the team ended for inactivity.

L4N V2

The second version of Lusitt4nos Nation founded in 02/10/2025, titled "The Comeback" was a second chance for Lusitt4nos Nation, they invited some of their old players, in this era soon after GUSTA was invited to the main roster, disaster struck, the team broke down, everyone left except the founders, later, F3nix Nation was created, founded by V1PER and GUSTA, then, the old players from L4N were invited to the main team, but jota? and W1TAPZ, definetly not happy with the situation, betrayed the team, and F3nix Nation era ended.

L4N V3

The last and current version of Lusitt4nos Nation, where the old main roster players from L4N v1 and v2 were invited, and coming with new players too, you can see the members of each era of Lusitt4nos Nation in "Hall of Memories".

L4N V4

Stable

With the end of the third era, the most stable era yet, despite some internal challenges the roster returned to full activity. Founders jota and feupas, alongside the co-founder V1PER, decided it was time to elevate the team to a professional standard. Recognizing that their previous identity was too similar to a world-class Counter Strike team, they made the bold move to abandon the old logo. This era marks the birth of a unique, professional, and more stable Lusitt4nos Nation, built on originality and a commitment to excellence.
